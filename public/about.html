About Me

Here is my stream of consciousness on how I got here:

I'm a Brit that is living in NYC and working for Reaktor doing Talent Acquisition. I completely fell into recruitment... after doing a masters in Psychology, I wasn't very clued up on how to search for jobs, or what jobs were even out there!

After a year stint in corporate communications in Brighton, UK, I returned to London without a job and was wondering what to do. That is when I got a phone call from an old colleague of mine, who found my CV on a job board, asking me to come in for an interview. I wasn't doing anything else, so I went.

I was offered the job, but honestly, I had no idea what recruitment was, or what I'd be doing, or really anything about what kinds of tasks I'd be doing. I moved to another recruitment agency, then to NYC, then to another... after five years in agency recruitment, the pandemic took its toll and I lost my job. I did a couple of years out of recruitment, before returning to internal recruitment at Reaktor.

I've been working at Reaktor for almost two years now, and have been enjoying being internal for sure. I recruit for all different roles: marketing, account management, design, engineering.

Working with and recruiting so many engineers started to feel like living in France and not speaking any French. So, I thought to myself, why don't I learn to code in my spare time, and Emma, my 'Reaktor Buddy' pointed me in the direction of this online course.

When I was picking my A-levels, my IT teacher begged me to do IT at A-Level but I said "no, it was full of boys"... Now I find myself back here, years later refreshing myself on html and looking forward to the next modules. :)

That's all, thanks for reading.
